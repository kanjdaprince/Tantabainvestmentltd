EXPORT/IMPORT

Tantaba Investments Ltd, your partner for comprehensive export and import logistics solutions. We understand that international trade can be complex and challenging, which is why we offer a range of logistics services to help you manage your exports and imports efficiently and effectively

Our export and import logistics services include:

  • Freight Forwarding: We offer air, sea, and road freight forwarding services for your exports and imports. With our extensive network of carriers and agents, we can transport your cargo anywhere in the world.
  • Customs Clearance: We handle all the necessary customs clearance procedures, ensuring that your cargo is in compliance with international regulations. Our experienced professionals work closely with customs officials to expedite the clearance process and minimize delays.
  • Warehousing and Distribution: We offer warehousing and distribution solutions for your cargo, providing you with safe and secure storage facilities and efficient distribution channels.
  • Documentation: We handle all the necessary documentation for your exports and imports, including bills of lading, invoices, and other required paperwork.
  • Cargo Insurance: We offer cargo insurance solutions to protect your cargo against loss, damage, or theft during transportation
    • At Tantaba Investments ltd, we are committed to providing our customers with exceptional service and support. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to ensuring that your project cargo is handled with the utmost care and attention to detail. We also use advanced technology and modern facilities to ensure that your cargo is tracked and monitored throughout its journey.
